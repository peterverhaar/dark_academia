I couldn't decide if the writing was dry or whether it was cleverly done and I sided with it was done on purpose to make you think and I actually love it more now and I did when I finished. I liked the way the characters all had an interest vibe to them and they shouldn't have worked well together but the writing was so well done that it made sense when it should not have.Dang I am sold! I need more books like this.4.5 starsThank you @kensingtonbooks for my gifted copy.
